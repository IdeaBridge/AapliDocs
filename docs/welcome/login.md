## Aapli Admin Panel: Account Login  
1.	Open a web browser on your device and go to https://admin-dev.aapli.app
2.	Enter your aapli account login credentials, including your e-mail address and password.
    - If you have created more than one Aapli website with your e-mail address, you will also get the option to choose the Aapli domain you'd like to log into.
3.	Wait for the Cloudflare verification to be completed.
4.	Click on the "Continue" button to log into your aapli account, at which point you will be directed to your homepage.
